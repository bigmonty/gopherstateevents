How to Use This Site: Cross-Country Meet Director

The GSE© web portal for managing cross-country meets is designed to make the process of managing meets managed by GSE© a clean and streamlined process. It should be noted that, for those that are not comfortable navigating web sites, you may also choose to manage your meet(s) via email.

Managing My Meet(s)
To manage one of your meets scored by GSE©, select the meet that you want to manage from the "Select Meet" dropdown on your home page. From here you will be able to manage the basic meet data, the races included in this meet, and the teams participating in the meet. Specific instructions for these tasks are included here.
  • Managing General Meet Information
    To edit the basic meet information for the meet, click on the "Edit This" link from within the frame containing that meet's general information.
  • Managing Races
    To edit the races for this meet, click on the "Edit This" link from within the frame containing that meet's race information. From there you can add a race, delete a race, or edit information for existing races.
  • Managing Participating Teams
    To edit the teams participating in the meet, click on the "Edit This" link from within the frame containing that meet's participating teams. From here you may edit the information on existing teams that are participating in your meet, or remove a team from your meet. To perform either of these actions, simply click on the team in question and work from the "Edit Teams" frame near the top right of the page.
    You may also insert teams into your meet but those teams must first exist in our teams database. If they are in the database they will show up in the "Insert A Team Into This Meet". If they do not show up in the dropdown you will have to add them to the database.
    To add a team to our database, fill out the information in the "Add Team to Database" frame on the page. In order to do this, the coach must exist in our coaches database. If they do, you can select them from the "Coach" dropdown. If they do not appear their, you will have to add them to the database.
Managing My Account
  • Adding a meet
    To add a meet that you would like scored by GSE©, select the "Add a Meet" item from the "Select Task" dropdown and fill out the ensuing form.
  • Editing My Info
    It is important to keep your contact information up to date to facilitate communications between yourself, GSE© staff, and your participating teams. To do this, select "Edit My Info" in the "Select Task" dropdown on your home page. Make whatever changes are required and click the "Save Changes" button at the bottom of the form.
  • Changing My Login
    You may change your login information any time and as often as you wish. To do this, select the appropriate item in the "Select Task" dropdown on your home page and fill out the ensuing form.
