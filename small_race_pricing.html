Small Race Pricing

At Gopher State Events, we believe there is a place in the fitness world for the smallish races, especially community-based events.  We want to help ensure that these events continue to thrive.

Pricing to Accommodate Event Size

Our small-race pricing plan is AUTOMATICALLY applied to any event if it is a better "deal" than our normal pricing plan.  This usually happens when participation levels are less than 90 to 115 participants.

No Change in Features

Our full menu of features applies to every event we time, regardless of size.  This includes finish line pictures for free download, individual email/text message results within minutes (usually seconds) of finishing, and everything else we provide for the mega event.
